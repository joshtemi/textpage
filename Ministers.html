Pastor Felix Omolayo Osunkiyesi

My name is Pastor Felix Omolayo Osunkiyesi, married with four children. I was born on March 26th, 1965, in Ondo Kingdom, Ondo State to an Anglican family. ... My education took me from elementary to college as a general Agriculturalist. I came to the United States in 1995. I joined an American Church known as True Vine Christian Worship Center in which I was an usher, a member of trusty board, and a minister for 10 years. I joined Christ Apostolic Church Salvation Center in 2006 and became a member in 2008. Since day one of my attending the church, I was introduced to the founder Pastor Dr. H.O Ilufoye. I became an usher that very year, a Deacon in 2009, Evangelist in 2015, and finally a Pastor in 2020. My main focus in the kingdom, is to see the children of God fulfilling the purposes of God in their lives and expanding God's kingdom on earth.

Head Pastor- Hezekiah

Alfred Abiodun Aremo was born in Jan 2nd, 1959 in Arigidi Akoko ,Akoko North West of Ondo state to the family of Mr & Mrs Michael Aremo ... I was born into the Anglican family but joined Christ Apostolic Church in 1980 in Oyin Akoko. I moved to Okeigbo in 1985 due to transfer as a Primary school teacher from Oyin Akoko. I became a choir in CAC,Okeiye Okeigbo and an Elder in October,1986.Meanwhile I was the secretary of Light of the world (Egbe Imola Aye) and later became the Youth Leader for Okeigbo Zone for 6years.In 1987 I got married to Motunrayo Oyinlola Aremo in, Okeigbo. In 1999 October, I moved to Ondo town for my children to have better education. In the year 2009 September 17th I moved to United States of America In 2013 Feb, I was ordained as an Evangelist in Christ Apostolic Church,north America when serving in CAC SALVATION CENTER,BALTIMORE. On August 20th 2020 I was ordained as a Pastor in CAC North America in Latunde Region. I am happily married with 2 daughters Taiwo and Kehinde born in 1988.
